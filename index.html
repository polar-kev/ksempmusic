Note to Self!

New Single! Oct. 9, 2020

Check the details in the album art, brought to you by @voske_art

Shout out to Adam Jones for his magic touch in production.

Note to Self started off as a song I made for myself to help me get through the unfortunate end of a relationship. After listing to it over and over and vibing to it in my apartment, I decided to share it with the world. This one's from the heart. If it speaks to you, let me know!

    • K Semp

    Kevin Semple, also known as K Semp, is a hip hop recording artist, beatmaker, producer and dancer from Canada. From politics, stories of past relationships, and motivational mantras, to boastful lyrics over booming 808s, K Semp's music showcases a wide variety of styles, highlighting an appreciation for the roots of hip hop as well as its evolution.

    K Semp is always on the road looking for inspiration. Step into his world and you're sure to find something you didn't know you were looking for.
