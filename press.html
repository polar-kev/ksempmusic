Bio

Kevin Semple, better known as K Semp, is a hip hop recording artist and beatmaker based out of Canada. After releasing his first single, "Thinking of You", K Semp returned with the 5 song EP, "Now You Know". From politics, relationships, and motivational mantras, to boastful lyrics over booming 808s, K Semp's music showcases a wide variety of styles, highlighting an appreciation for the roots of hip hop as well as its evolution.

K Semp also takes pride in his ability to craft instrumentals across various genres. Having produced 4 out of the 5 instrumentals off his own EP, K Semp is solidifying his position as an up and coming artist to keep an eye on.
